Standard Operating Procedure - Document & Billing Requests:
1. Invoices & Financials:
   - Invoice Inquiries and billing disputes must capture invoice numbers and shipment reference IDs.
   - Billing disputes (e.g., detention charges, accessorial fee disputes) must be classified as High priority and flagged for Accounts Receivable review.
2. Document Requests:
   - Requests for Proof of Delivery (POD), Bill of Lading (BOL), or Rate Confirmations are classified as Low to Normal priority.
   - Direct staff to email requested PDF documentation to the verified contact email provided by the caller.
