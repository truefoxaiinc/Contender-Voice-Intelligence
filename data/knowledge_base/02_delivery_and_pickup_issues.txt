Standard Operating Procedure - Delivery & Pickup Issues:
1. Priority Classification Guidelines:
   - Urgent: Any safety hazards, vehicle accidents, driver emergencies, or critical line-down situations.
   - High: Same-day delivery delays, missed pickup windows, damaged freight upon arrival, or dock refusal.
   - Normal/Low: Advance notice of minor delays, future schedule changes, or routine location confirmation.
2. Immediate Next Actions:
   - For reported damages, direct staff to inspect photo proof and delay POD signing until verified.
   - For missed pickup/delivery windows, direct staff to check driver GPS and immediately notify carrier dispatch.
   - Ensure shipment reference numbers/BOL numbers are extracted whenever available.
